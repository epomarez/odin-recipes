A delicious pizza with pineapple

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 portion pizza dough
  • 2 tbsp pizza sauce
  • 300g shaved ham
  • 1 x tin pineapple pieces in natural juice, drained
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Roll out pizza dough and place on hot pizza stone or baking tray.

  2. Using the back of a spoon, spread the pizza sauce evenly across the dough.

  3. Place the ham on the dough then add the pineapple.

  4. Scatter cheese across the top and bake for 20-25 mins or until golden brown.
